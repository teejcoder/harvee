# Overview

## What is this?

A time tracking and invoicing tool — a Harvest clone.

## Who is it for?

Me: an independent contract developer who needs to track billable hours and turn them into invoices.

## What does it do?

It tracks time and turns that time into invoices.

You set up work in a simple hierarchy: a **client**, one or more **projects** under that client, and **tasks** within each project. When you're working, you start a timer against a task, stop it when you're done, and add notes about what you did. Timed entries can be edited if you forgot to start or stop the clock.

When it's time to bill, you click a button and the app generates an invoice: the client becomes the billable party, and the project and its tasks show up as line items. You can edit the invoice before sending, then export it to PDF.

There's also a historical view — a calendar you can browse by month, week, or day — so you can look back at how your time was spent.

## What does it NOT do?

It does not track expenses. It does not do anything beyond tracking time and generating invoices from that time.
